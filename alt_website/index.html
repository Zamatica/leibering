What are Dimension Products?

Dimension products are the wood components required by furniture and cabinet makers, hardwood flooring Installers and construction companies to build their specific products.
Leibering Dimension provides FSC Certified components for all of your design build projects.

Whether you are building homes or offices, or filling those homes and offices with wood products. With our knowledge and highly skilled craftsman and staff we will deliver with quality, superior customer service, on time and on budget every time.
