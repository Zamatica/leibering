Our Sustainability

Leibering Dimension purchases green lumber and uses one of the 4 kilns to dry and process it. Unique in the industry, Leibering Dimension is one of the few companies that has the capability of taking a product from harvest to completion. We think you'll find Leibering Dimension easy to work with and a high-quality on-time supplier!

The Leibering Companies are completely vertically integrated. We have the ability to plant trees, select and harvest for regeneration and transport logs to the sawmill, which in turn provides lumber for the dimension plant.

From Roughout lumber to our beautiful hardwood flooring, our success to attributed to our great workforce and our ability to control the production process from start to finish. Our attention to detail is the heart of everything we do.
